# Advanced Topics

## What You Will Learn

- Topological sort: Kahn's algorithm and DFS-based
- Tries: prefix trees, autocomplete, word search
- Two heaps: median finding, balanced partitions
- K-way merge: merging K sorted sequences
- Bitwise XOR: finding unique elements, bit tricks
- Cyclic sort: numbers 1-n, missing/duplicate detection
- Merge intervals: overlapping range problems
- Prefix sum: O(1) range sum queries

## Why This Matters

These are specialized patterns that appear in medium and hard problems. Each solves a specific class of problems efficiently. Mastering them rounds out your algorithm toolkit for interview readiness.
