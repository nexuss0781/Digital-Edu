# Greedy Algorithms

## What You Will Learn

- Greedy choice property: local optimal leads to global optimal
- When greedy works vs when it fails
- Interval scheduling and activity selection
- Greedy vs DP: how to distinguish
- Proof of correctness for greedy approaches

## Why This Matters

Greedy algorithms make the best choice at each step without reconsidering. When the greedy choice property holds, they are simpler and faster than DP. Knowing when to use greedy vs DP is a critical interview skill.
