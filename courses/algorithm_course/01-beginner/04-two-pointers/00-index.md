# Two Pointers

## What You Will Learn

- The two pointers pattern: using two indices to traverse data
- Three variants: opposite ends, same direction, fast-slow pointers
- When sorted data enables O(n) pair finding
- How two pointers reduces O(n²) to O(n)
- Decision tree for choosing the right variant

## Why This Matters

Two pointers is one of the 17 core patterns that solve 90% of interview problems. It teaches you to think about data traversal efficiency — instead of checking every pair (O(n²)), use the structure of sorted data to eliminate possibilities with each step.
